Resolution is as follows:
1. Determine **SPELL COST.**
2. Adjust relevant **Emotions** by the cost.
3. Roll d100 under **Emotions** + **Magical Sync**. **M-Sync** defaults to 25.

>[!callout] When do I roll?
>Anything an *average* person could do with effort, a Magi will *always* automatically succeed.
>
>Magical girls typically automatically succeed on common or everyday tasks related to their professions or anywhere where they could be considered a "professional". Even if it's difficult or more prone to mistakes. Magical active emotions *twist* reality to better fit your needs.
>
>---
>That being said, *whenever* you use magic or do something magical in nature, if there is a risk or a possible cost of action, *roll for it*. This is especially true in [[THE LOBBY |The Lobby]], where magic is the standard.
