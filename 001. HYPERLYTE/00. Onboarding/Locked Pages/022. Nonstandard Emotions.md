The majority of spells fall under the *7 Deadly sins*, but some resonate with **non-standard emotions**. 

Specifically, there are *2* nonstandard emotions. **Apathy** and **Treason**. A Magi should *never* feel apathy, as this is *anti-magic*. Likewise a magical girl's *treason* will destroy all the people she works with. These two emotions are **forbidden resonances**.
