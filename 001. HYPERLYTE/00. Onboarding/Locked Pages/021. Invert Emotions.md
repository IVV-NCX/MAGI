#  Invert Emotions
Emotions are ||**Absolute**|| for any magical girl. This means that any *positive* emotion can be changed into it's *negative* form or vice versa. 


## Broken Inversions
Some spells *do not* have an invert for their exact emotional resonance. All M.A.I.D codex spells have an invert, but not all MAGI spells have an invert. Imagine a spell whose resonance is **gloom//melancholy**. Gloom with no cause. There are many cases where a spell that resonates in this form would have no valid inverse.

>[!question]+ Doesn't Gloom have an inverse?
>Correct. Gloom has many inverses, from joy to happiness, to elation. However, Gloom, like all other emotions is a *collective* of emotions. Some of these specific emotions might not have inverses. So remember:
>>[!important]
>>*A spell always has a sin. However, the specific emotions it relies on may not have inverses. Never cast an inverse of a spell if you're not sure of it's inverse resonances.*


These are known as *false spells*. Spells with emotions outside the human boundary.
