*"True knowledge is true magic."*

The Pavilion is an exception to the M.A.I.D program's standard procedures. Originally founded by "Xiao", the Pavilion was an institution dedicated towards introducing traditional medicines with the modern medicines. When Xiao later became a magical girl under **SOUHAIT**'s influence, she agreed to work under M.A.I.D, and integrated the Pavilion.

Now, the Pavilion strives to further combine traditional and modern medicines through magic and the study of magic.

---

Unlike most factions, the Pavilion specializes in *magic sync*, or the ability to utilize magic without emotional interference through a process more popularly known as cultivation. This involves targeted studying, proceeded by intensive mental training to master the logic underlying a spell without emotional resonances. As a result, Pavilion magical girls tend to be less emotional, and more capable at casting spells than their peers.
