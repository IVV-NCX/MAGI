**The Lobby** is where magical girls dream. This space can blur, and almost always, into a simple dream. Typical things. Flying around, falling off a cliff, naked in a classroom. However, when certain conditions are met- the lobby becomes accessible, regardless of being a true magical girl. Time is dilated here- every magical girl who meets the conditions and sleeps within the twenty-four hour duration of each lobby enters at the same time, and leaves in the number of hours they would wake up in. The Lobby correlates to the lunar calendar. 

The Lobby is a fairly pretty space. Desks, chairs, small buildings and whatnot, built by magical girls with the little bit of magic they can use whilst in the Lobby. You'll often see magi chatting about the nightmares they met that day. Although... it does get auto-censored if the knowledge would affect the timescale. Who, or how this works is unknown.

Every 60 minutes, a gate opens. The gate to the deep dive.
